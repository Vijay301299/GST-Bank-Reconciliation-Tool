Why this exists

Built by a practitioner, not a product team.

Every month, GST reconciliation comes down to the same thing: two spreadsheets, one from the portal and one from the books, and hours spent matching invoice numbers by eye. I built this tool for my own consultancy work first — a way to run that match reliably, see exactly why something didn't tie out, and export a clean report without opening a single third-party dashboard.

It's now free for any practitioner or business to use. The matching logic is a deterministic rule engine — every result is explainable. AI is entirely optional, used only if you choose to add an API key, and only for plain-language observations layered on top of results the rule engine already produced.

What it does

Two reconciliations, one tool.

GST Reconciliation

Match GSTR-2B / portal data against your books by invoice number, GSTIN, date, and tax amount. Get duplicate detection on both sides, configurable tolerances, and a clear exception list for anything that doesn't tie out.

Bank Reconciliation

Match your bank statement against your ledger by date and amount. Paste, upload a CSV/Excel file, or drag and drop — then export a clean, audit-ready PDF report.

How your data is handled

Nothing you upload ever leaves your browser.

There is no server and no database behind this tool. Every file you paste, upload, or drag in is parsed entirely by JavaScript running in your own browser tab.

If you choose to add an Anthropic API key for AI-generated observations, only a small statistical summary and a sample of mismatched rows are sent — directly from your browser to Anthropic's API, never through a server of ours. This is entirely optional; the tool is fully functional without it.

Nothing is logged, stored, or retained. Close the tab, and it's gone.
